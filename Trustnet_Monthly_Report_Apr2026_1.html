D13 Precision/Recall Model — April 2026

Assesses audience model accuracy
Methodology. Precision measures what proportion of users in each audience genuinely exhibit the target behaviour. Recall measures what proportion of all qualifying users on the site the audience successfully captures. F1 is the harmonic mean of both. A score above 70% indicates a commercially viable audience for targeting and activation.
