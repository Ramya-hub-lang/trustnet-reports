About this Fund

Scottish Mortgage Investment Trust invests in a portfolio of global equities, predominantly growth companies. The trust has a long-term investment horizon of at least 5 years and aims to identify exceptional businesses worldwide.

Managed by Tom Slater and Lawrence Burns at Baillie Gifford, the trust is known for its high-conviction approach and willingness to hold unlisted companies.
